The Legend

About Prabhas

Born Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju on October 23, 1979 in Chennai, Prabhas grew up in a family deeply rooted in Telugu cinema. His uncle, the legendary Krishnam Raju, inspired him to step in front of the camera.

He debuted in 2002 with Eeswar and broke through with the action-romance Varsham in 2004. But it was S.S. Rajamouli's epic Baahubali saga that transformed him from a beloved regional star into India's first true pan-national superstar, beloved across every language and state.

His journey is one of patience, dedication, and mythic ambition — spending nearly 5 years on the Baahubali franchise alone, proving that the biggest rewards come to those who dare to dream on an epic scale.
